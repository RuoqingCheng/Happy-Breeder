User's manual

Basics

  • You get two kittens when you first time log in!
  • Please feed them with food, clean them by scooping, and treat them with cat grass and medicine regularly to keep them happy and healthy.
  • Brush cats can get furballs (fortune)! You can also visit others and get furballs from their cats

What do the values of the kittens mean?

  • FURBALL: your cats can generate fur balls with time. You need furballs to purchase things in the store.
  • FOOD: showing if your cats need to be fed. You can use items such as milk and can food to increase this value.
  • CLEARNESS: showing if your cats need to be cleaned. You can use “scoop” button to increase this value.
  • HEALTH: it will decrease if you don’t take care of your cats well.
  • AGE: pets can mate with each other when pets is age 2 and the heart appears.

Breeder's Tip

  • If you get cats with wings, congradulations! If you use them for breeding, you have a higher chance to get offsprings of other breeds.
  • If you want to maintain the purity of your breeds, breed within the same kind.
  • Cats price depends on its purity, age, and breed.
